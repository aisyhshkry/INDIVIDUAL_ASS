MY JOURNEY OF EDUCATION

SEKOLAH KEBANGSAAN MERBAU SEMPAK

2010-2011

Sekolah Kebangsaan Merbau Sempak (SKMS) is my first primary school which located in Gombak District, Selangor. SMKS is situated 2.5km from Sungai Buloh Prison Complex where I lived before. As the children of prison officer, my friends and I go to the school by bus. I do remember that my bestfriend, Tisha and I were appointed to be the school prefect for the year 1 students. However, we could not spend more time together as I have to transferred to another school because there is new school opened which more close to the prison complex.

SEKOLAH KEBANGSAAN DESA AMAN

2011-2012

Sekolah Kebangsaan Desa Aman (SEDESA) is a primary school in Selangor, Malaysia. It is founded on 16 September 2011 and it is the largest primary school by school assembly point in Selangor and Malaysia. The children of prison officer has been transferred to this new school including me, myself. It is a new school and we are the first batch of student who studied there. Unfortunately, I get only one year of experience there as my father has been transferred back to our hometown which is Kelantan.

SEKOLAH KEBANGSAAN ISMAIL PETRA 1

2012-2015

Sekolah Kebangsaan Ismail Petra 1 (SKIP1) is situated in Kota Bharu, Kelantan. It is located in Kompleks Sekolah-Sekolah Wakaf Mek Zainab Kota Bharu, Kelantan. It is named after the Honorable Sultan Ismail Petra, the 28th Sultan of Kelantan. I met new friends and they were being so nice to me even though we just know each other. Glad to share that I still contact with a few of my friends from this school up until now. Some of them even went to the same secondary school as mine and some up until the foundation life.

SEKOLAH MENENGAH KEBANGSAAN ISMAIL PETRA (INTEG)

2016-2020

Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Ismail Petra (SMKIP) also being name after the Honorable Sultan Ismail Petra, which also located in Kompleks Sekolah-Sekolah Wakaf Mek Zainab Kota Bharu, Kelantan. It is one of the Cluster School of Excellence (SKK) in Kelantan. I sat my PT3 here and pass with flying colors which is 10A and 1B. Then, I sat for my SPM and pass with 6A and 3B. For me, high school was the best memories for me and my friends as we spent a lot of time together more than our family.

PUSAT ASASI UITM DENGKIL

2021-2022

Pusat Asasi, or Centre of Foundation Studies (CFS), is located in Dengkil, Selangor. It provides a solid foundation for students to continue their degree programmes in UiTM and other institutions of higher learning both locally and internationally. The Centre offers five 5 programmes which are Foundation in Science, Foundation in Engineering, Foundation in Law, and Foundation in TESL. I am glad to say that I got the opportunity to studied in Foundation in Law for the total of two semesters before I applied to further studied in degree.

UNIVERSITI TEKNOLOGI MARA KELANTAN KAMPUS KOTA BHARU

2022-NOW

Universiti Teknologi Mara Cawangan Kelantan Kampus Kota Bharu is located in Kota Bharu, Kelantan. It is the second campus in Kelantan which the first one or known as the main campus is located in Machang. In this campus, we have only degree's student from three faculties which are Faculty of Science and Mathematics, Faculty of Business and Management and Faculty of Accounting. I am currently studying in year 2 of Bachelor of Business Administration (Hons) Business Economics. Unfortunately, I did not get to further my dreams in law but I am happy with my life now. I believe that something greater will happen to me in the future.
